Facts about dogs

Dogs have a sense of time. It's been proven that they know the difference between a hour and five. If conditioned to, they can predict future evvents, such as regular walk times.

A study wass conducted on dogs being away from their owners for varying hours and the studies show that dogs who were away from their owners the longest showed the greatest amount of affection!
